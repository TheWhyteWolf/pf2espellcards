Use offline

Everything you do — your characters and prepared spells — is saved on this device only, in your browser. Nothing is sent anywhere. Keep a copy so it always works, even with no signal:

“Save offline copy” downloads this whole spellbook as a single file you can open any time — no internet needed. “Install app” adds it to your home screen like a normal app (appears when your browser supports it).

Characters

Tap a character to switch to it. You can keep as many as you like — each remembers its own prepared spells.

Share & back up

Export your active character to a code you can save or send to a friend. To load one, paste a code below and tap Import — it's added as a new character.
